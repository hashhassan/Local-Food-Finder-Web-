Khan Nihari House

Khan Nihari House is one of the most well-known breakfast spots in Rawalpindi, famous for its traditional Paye Nihari. The restaurant has been serving customers for over 25 years and is a favourite among locals. The nihari is slow-cooked overnight and served hot every morning with freshly baked naan. The place is simple but always packed with customers early in the morning.

Apart from nihari, they also serve halwa puri, channay, and doodh pati. The seating is basic but the food quality is consistent and affordable. It is located in the busy Bhabra Bazaar area, which is easy to reach by public transport.

Restaurant Name Khan Nihari House
Famous For Paye Nihari, Halwa Puri
Location Bhabra Bazaar, Rawalpindi
